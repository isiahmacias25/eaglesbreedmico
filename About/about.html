About Us

Our Mission

The Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC is dedicated to promoting camaraderie, community service, and the spirit of freedom that comes with the open road. Our mission is to bring riders together, support charitable initiatives, and make a positive impact on our community.

Our History

Founded in 2022, the Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC has grown from a small group of passionate riders into a respected organization. The Miami County Chapter was established in 2024 to unite riders in the area, creating a space for fellowship and giving back to the community. Over the years, we’ve organized countless charity rides, supported local causes, and built a legacy of loyalty and service.

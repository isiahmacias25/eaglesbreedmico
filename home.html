Welcome to the official website of the Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC – Miami County, Ohio Chapter. We are a nonprofit motorcycle club dedicated to camaraderie, community service, and the open road. Our chapter is proud to support local initiatives, host charitable events, and bring together riders who share a passion for making a positive impact.

Explore our site to learn more about who we are, view our calendar of events, catch up on the latest news, and see highlights from our past rides and accomplishments. Sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about upcoming activities and ways to support our mission.

Together, we ride to make a difference.
